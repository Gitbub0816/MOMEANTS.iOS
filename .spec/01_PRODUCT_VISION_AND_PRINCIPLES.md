# Product Vision and Principles

Momeants is a memory-first social platform centered on meaningful relationships, life events, nostalgia, and important people. It is not a follower-count app, a viral discovery network, or a short-form dopamine feed. It is a place where a single photo can feel significant.

## Core emotional promise

Momeants should feel like opening a private, glowing box of real life. Every screen should tell the user: your memories matter, your people matter, and your life is not disposable content.

## Product pillars

### 1. One memory at a time
The primary feed presents one photo-centered Momeant at a time. Avoid dense grids as the main consumption mode. The user should feel present with one memory before moving to the next.

### 2. Relationships over followers
Momeants should prefer meaningful relationships over vanity networks. The system should understand mothers, fathers, siblings, partners, close friends, family circles, and emotionally important people.

### 3. Significance over virality
The platform should rank and resurface memories based on personal relevance, not raw public engagement.

### 4. Calm but alive
The app should feel cinematic, premium, warm, and responsive. It should not feel noisy, flat, gimmicky, or corporate.

### 5. Privacy as a product feature
Privacy must be obvious, reassuring, and present in the interaction model. A user should know who can see a Momeant before posting.

## Initial feature set

- Native account creation and sign-in.
- Profile creation.
- Single-photo Momeant posting.
- Captions.
- Audience selection.
- Important People.
- One-photo-at-a-time feed.
- Tap left/right navigation.
- Swipe reactions.
- Super-like / gold heart reaction.
- Close-circle sharing.
- Memory resurfacing.
- Basic notifications.
- Reporting and moderation.
- Settings and privacy controls.

## Deferred feature set

- Long video.
- Public creator monetization.
- Broad discovery tab.
- Complex live chat.
- Public follower marketplace.
- Heavy AI editing tools.

## Naming language

- A post is a `Momeant`.
- A collection of meaningful people is `Important People`.
- A high-value reaction is a `Gold Heart` or `Super Like`.
- Resurfaced memories are `Remembered` or `Brought Back`.
- Avoid cold labels like `content item`, `asset`, or `object` in user-facing copy.
